Our competition

We compete in NASA's annual Robot Mining Competition that is held at the Kennedy Space Center. The competition consists of engineering an autonomous robot that can navigate the arena, dig for minerals beneath the surface, and return the materials to a collection bin.

The competition aims to simulate the surface of the Moon in preperation for NASA's Artemis program, which aims to land astronauts on the Lunar surface by 2024.
